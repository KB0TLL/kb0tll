Our Repeaters

Our Club Operates Three Repeaters, call sign KBØTLL:

VHF – FM/Echolink – 147.075, offset +0.6 MHz, 141.3 tone – located approximately 4 miles north of Hillsboro, MO – On Echolink, search our call sign KBØTLL

VHF – C4FM Wires X – 147.105 +0.6 MHz, no tone – located at the 911 Tower, House Springs MO – Currently linked to the club’s Liguori Room.

UHF – FM – 442.425 MHz +5MHz 141.3 tone – Became operational Nov. 11, 2024 – located approximately 4 miles north of Hillsboro.

OTHER AFFILIATED REPEATERS

VHF – FM/C4FM Wires X – 146.775 MHz +600 100.0 tone – Owned by club member Gary KDØRIS. Buck Knob, Crystal City. Wires-X local only, currently no node connected.

6 Meters – FM – 52.950 MHz -1.7 MHz 192.8 tone – TX in Arnold, RX in Barnhart. AMC club.

Our Nets

Come join our NETS! Whether you’re a club member or not, you’re always welcome!

MON – Monday Night Emergency Practice Net – 147.075 repeater – 8pm Monday Night – We welcome REGULAR, SHORT-TIME, MOBILE/PORTABLE, and ECHOLINK check-ins.

MON – Monthly Emergency Simplex Net – 146.550 MHz FM Simplex – 1st Monday each month follows the Monday Night Emergency Practice Net. Alternate Frequencies: 146.580, 146.520.

MON – Monday Night Swap Net – 147.075 repeater – Follows the Emergency Practice Net, except on 1st Monday of the month.

TUE – The Brunch Bunch – Buck Knob Repeater – 146.775 100.0 tone – Tuesdays at 10am. Casual roundtable. Not an official club net.

WED – Come As You Are Net – 147.075 repeater – 9:00 – Casual roundtable.

TUE/THR/SAT – Old Geezer’s Net – 147.075 repeater – Starting around 10:05pm and finishing up around 11:00pm. Casual roundtable.

First Tuesday of each Month – The Jefferson County ARES NET 147.075 repeater – 6:30pm

FRI – TGIF (Fred) Net – 147.105 repeater – Friday Evenings at 7pm.
